Heavily inspired by, palette taken from, and a few pixels copied from Space Scifi Rpg Tiles 48x48 by Hyptosis.

The energy crystal is from Sci-Fi Blue Pillar by FunwithPixels.

The table is from and the computers  are based on [LPC] Skorpio's SciFi Sprite Pack by Skorpio.

Energy crystal and table got both adapted to the new palette.

Embedding a crystal into a table is based on an idea of Psychronic.

Contains a small energy crystal meant as inventory item.
